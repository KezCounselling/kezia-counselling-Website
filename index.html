About Me

Hi, I'm Kezia.

🎓 Counselling Psychologist

I'm a counselling psychologist and I truly believe that every person holds within them the capacity for growth, resilience, and healing — sometimes we just need a little support to find it.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and deeply personal. I draw from evidence-based methods including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), mindfulness, and person-centred therapy — adapting each session to your unique needs, not a one-size-fits-all formula.

Whether you're dealing with anxiety, navigating a difficult life transition, or simply feeling stuck — I'm here to walk alongside you, at your pace.

Questions

FAQs

What happens in the first session? +
The first session is about getting to know each other. I'll ask about what's been on your mind, what brought you here, and what you're hoping to gain. There's no pressure to share everything at once — we go at your pace.
How long does therapy usually take? +
It depends on your goals and what you're working through. Some people find 6–8 sessions helpful; others prefer ongoing support over a longer period. We'll review progress together regularly.
Is everything I share kept confidential? +
Yes, absolutely. Confidentiality is a cornerstone of our work together. The only exceptions are rare legal or ethical situations where safety is at risk — which I'll always explain clearly from the start.
Do you offer online sessions? +
Yes! I offer online sessions via secure video call — so you can choose whatever feels most comfortable for you.
I'm nervous about starting. Is that normal? +
Completely. Most people feel a little anxious before their first session — that's a very human response. My role is to make you feel safe and at ease from the very first moment.
